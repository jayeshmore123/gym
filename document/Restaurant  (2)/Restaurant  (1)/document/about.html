About us

Welcome to Pranav's Restaurant! At Pranav's Restaurant , we believe that dining is more than just a meal—it’s an experience. Located in the heart of Shrigonda, our restaurant combines a passion for great food with an inviting atmosphere to create a memorable culinary journey.


Our story

Founded in 2015 by Pranav's, Pranav's Restaurant was born from a love of so many people. With a background in Inspiration of grand mother, Pranav's set out to create a place where Goal "every dish tells a story" and "traditional recipes meet modern techniques".


Our Philosophy

We are dedicated to using only the freshest, locally-sourced ingredients to craft our dishes. Each menu item is thoughtfully prepared to highlight the best flavors and deliver an unforgettable dining experience. Our chefs are passionate about specific Cooking Style, and they strive to infuse creativity and quality into every plate.


Stay Connected

For the latest updates on our menu, events, and specials, follow us on Social Media Platforms or subscribe to our newsletter. We also love hearing from our guests, so feel free to leave us a review or contact us directly.
